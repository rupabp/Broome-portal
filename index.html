About ASRS v1.1

Developed by WHO for adults 18+. Part A (6 items) is the validated screener — score of 4+ suggests ADHD symptoms warranting follow-up. Part B gives additional detail. Not a standalone diagnosis — reviewed by Dr. Patel or Dr. Carter.
